2. <span data-ttu-id="291b1-106">Rimuovere qualsiasi [indirizzo di inoltro](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/admin/email/configure-email-forwarding) sospetto a livello di cassetta postale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="291b1-106">Remove any suspicious [forwarding addresses](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/admin/email/configure-email-forwarding) set at the mailbox level.</span></span>
