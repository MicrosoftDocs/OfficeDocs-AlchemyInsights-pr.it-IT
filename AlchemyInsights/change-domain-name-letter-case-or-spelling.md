    <span data-ttu-id="8512e-105">Per istruzioni più dettagliate, vedere [Come rimuovere un dominio](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/admin/get-help-with-domains/remove-a-domain?view=o365-worldwide).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8512e-105">For for more detailed instructions, see [How to remove a domain](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/admin/get-help-with-domains/remove-a-domain?view=o365-worldwide).</span></span>
