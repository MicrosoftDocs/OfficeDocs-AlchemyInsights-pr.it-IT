# <a name="mail-flow-rules-also-known-as-transport-rules"></a>Regole del flusso di posta (note anche come regole di trasporto)

- Panoramica generale delle regole del flusso di posta: [regole del flusso di posta (regole di trasporto) in Exchange Online](https://technet.microsoft.com/library/jj919238.aspx)

- Configurare le regole del flusso [di posta: procedure della regola del flusso di posta in Exchange Online](https://technet.microsoft.com/library/dn600436.aspx)

- Creare, modificare ed eliminare le regole del flusso di posta: [gestire le regole del flusso di posta](https://technet.microsoft.com/library/jj657505.aspx)

Per ulteriori informazioni, vedere [Get-TransportRule](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/module/exchange/policy-and-compliance/get-transportrule) (visualizzazione), [New-TransportRule](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/module/exchange/policy-and-compliance/new-transportrule) (Create), [Remove-TransportRule](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/module/exchange/policy-and-compliance/remove-transportrule) (Delete), [Set-TransportRule](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/module/exchange/policy-and-compliance/set-transportrule) (modify existing), [Disable-TransportRule](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/module/exchange/policy-and-compliance/disable-transportrule) (Disable existing) e [Enable-TransportRule](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/module/exchange/policy-and-compliance/enable-transportrule) (attiva esistente).
